Frequently Asked Questions

What types of items can I donate? +
We accept a wide range of new or gently used items including furniture, electronics, clothing, books, and more. All items must be in good working condition and meet our quality guidelines.
How do I know my donation is going to a legitimate organization? +
All organizations on our platform go through a thorough verification process. We check their non-profit status, review their documentation, and maintain ongoing monitoring of their activities.
Is my donation tax-deductible? +
Yes, donations to verified non-profit organizations are typically tax-deductible. You'll receive a donation receipt that you can use for tax purposes.
